Game Description

I press my forehead to the window,
peering through the glass,

Game Tips

1. Get comfortable & play at night
  - The game won't take too long, around an hour, so you only need to free 1 hour of your time in the evening for the greatest emotional
  experience!

2. Pay attention to the story.
  - This technically isn't a slow burn, but you'll miss key parts of the story if you don't pay enough attention, your connection with Ember is
  important too!

3. Come back for the story!
  - Hyuno is currently serving in the military, and he'll be back around October 2026 (estimation).

What I love about Expedition

I love the theme of this game, it's really relatable with a lovely message, I feel like this'll be very relatable to many people, the journey shows the pain instead of the highlights the choice and fate in Expedition makes you feel powerless, yet still alive in the world.
