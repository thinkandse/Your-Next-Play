Game Description

"You lived through hell once. Are you sure you wanna live through hell again?"
Sever is a SLOW BURN single-player story game about connecting and letting go. (Only the first part is out)

Game Tips

1. Use the voice chat!
  - The game is 10x more immersive with the voice chat feature, don't forget it. Talking out the dialogue & poems makes it feel much more real. Not to mention there is a cool metaphor that only voice chat users get to experience.

2. Second Cut
  - Make sure you stay around for Cut 2 that will be releasing in 2026!

3. Know that it's a slow burn
  - Tofu's ability of make a quick slow burn (~1 hours) is incredible, use headphones and pay attention to the story!

What I love about Sever

The connection with Iris feels so genuine, like it feel like it was based off a true story. The art direction literally is so unique, play it yourself to see what I mean.

I also have to talk about how deep the symbolism and metaphors are in this game, from the poems all the way to your actions and voice chat, I won't spoil anything here but there are so many examples of that!

Lastly, voice chat is used so well.

Parents' Guide

As of right now, this is all ages, however, fans so speculate darker themes in Second + Third cut, though the First cut is fully safe for all ages.
